About Sea Pure

Sea Pure started as a family‑run experiment to fix one simple problem: shutki quality is unpredictable once it reaches the city.

We keep the product list short, build direct relationships with a few coastal suppliers, and focus on consistent batches instead of hundreds of random items.

  • Curated catalog of everyday shutki items.
  • Batch‑wise labeling so you know exactly what you bought.
  • Small storage cycles to keep stock moving and fresh.

How we source & dry

Fish is purchased at coastal landing stations in Cox’s Bazar and Kuakata during early morning auctions and sorted the same day.

We use traditional sun‑drying on raised bamboo beds with covered netting, followed by moisture checks and quick packing in Dhaka.

  • Visual sorting to remove damaged or stale fish.
  • Sun‑drying on clean raised platforms away from ground dust.
  • Final odor and texture check before packaging.
